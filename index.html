The Challenge

Gynecological suturing demands sustained focus, fine motor control, and consistent technique. Operating rooms thrive on speed and standardization—yet suturing can be time‑intensive and variable across cases and teams.

Time

Manual suturing can extend operative time, impacting schedule flow and resource utilization.

Complexity

High skill requirements and variable anatomy make repeatable performance challenging.

Consistency

Variations in technique can influence outcomes, training needs, and quality metrics.

Our Solution

A semi-automatic suturing device purpose-built for gynecological procedures—designed to elevate control, streamline workflow, and support consistent, high‑quality outcomes.
